What Is OCB Coin?

Initial Coin Offering (ICO) is a method of crowdfunding a new cryptocurrency in the market. Startup companies use it as a source of capital to raise funds for their new projects. This is done by offering to sell a percentage of the newly issued cryptocurrency to investors at a reduced price. This reduced initial price is used to draw in sponsors and investors who will benefit more when the cryptocurrency gains more value.

The Initial Coin Offer gives investors the opportunity to enjoy an early bird rate in investment.
